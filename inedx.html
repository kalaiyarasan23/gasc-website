College Infrastructure

The campus spread over a vast area of 19.13 acres, in which there are two buildings. Main building has The Principal’s Chamber, Administrative Office, Examination Cell, National Service Scheme (NSS) room, the department of Tamil, English, Mathematics, Physics and Computer Science have separate chambers laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Computer Science, 39 classrooms, and library with 7,000 books, adequate Furniture like book cases Cupboards , Chair, Table, Desk Benches for the students utility. Our administrative office equipped with Computers, Scanners, Printers and Xerox facilities, The basic amenities such as 28 lavatories, 7 Water tanks with playgrounds, A seminar hall with 300 seating capacity, Audio, Video, a projector, monitor in a windy ambience in which all departments celebrate their academic events. The college is in continuous pursuit of educational excellence by its thriving efforts to provide a holistic learning environment to the students’ community with state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities.
